# Data

Practitioner coaching documentation from the Spring 2026 cohort (N=18) is currently under embargo.

## What will be released here

Upon completion of ethics review and participant consent:

- Anonymised per-client arc summaries (P01–P18)
- Cohort synthesis document
- Group session pattern summaries
- Coding schema and inter-rater materials

## Embargo conditions

Data release is contingent on:

1. **Institutional ethics review** — IRB or equivalent approval obtained
2. **Retroactive research consent** — explicit written consent from all 18 Spring 2026 cohort participants, administered by a third party not involved in facilitation, including the right to withdraw without consequence

Any participant who declines consent will have their data excluded. The released dataset will reflect the final consented N.

## Timeline

Ethics and consent processes are in progress as of April 2026. Expected release: prior to CHI 2027 submission (target October 2026).
